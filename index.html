Hey there,
I guess this is my safe place.
You know this place in a busy market? A tiny stand, not really a proper store, but enough to exist?
This is that place, my stand somewhere on the internet.

I feel like in today’s world, or maybe it’s me, Ideas just come and go. We’re exposed to so much information, which our head struggles to retain. I’m passionate about a new thing and move to a new one before I make sense of the first. If I only had a place, where I can package a thought, a research, as a complete, coherent text and archive it for good.

Here’s me

I hope to contribute to a world where people care. Where they’re interested in the mechanisms around them, created either intentionally or not. Where beauty, aesthetics, art have a significance, where people strive to master different skills. Where people look for ways to make a better future. Where people care about each other.
I care, and want to do something about it.
